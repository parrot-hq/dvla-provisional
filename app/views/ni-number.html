What is your National Insurance number?

Your National Insurance number helps DVLA verify your identity against government records. Providing it speeds up your application.

{{ govukInsetText({ text: "If you are under 16 or have not yet received your National Insurance number, you can skip this step." }) }}

You can find your National Insurance number on:

  • your payslip or P60
  • a letter from HMRC
  • your personal tax account on GOV.UK
