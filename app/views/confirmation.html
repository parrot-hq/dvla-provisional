What you can do once your licence arrives

  • book your theory test at the earliest opportunity - you must pass the theory test before you can book a practical driving test
  • start driving lessons with a DVSA-approved instructor, or practise with a qualified driver aged 21 or over who has held a full licence for at least 3 years
  • display L plates on the front and rear of any vehicle you drive

You cannot drive on motorways unless accompanied by an approved driving instructor in a dual-control car.
