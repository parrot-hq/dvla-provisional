Apply for your first provisional driving licence

Use this service to get a provisional driving licence for a car, motorbike, moped or other vehicle.

Before you start

To apply you must:

  • be at least 15 years and 9 months old
  • live in England, Scotland or Wales
  • have been resident in Great Britain for at least 185 days in the past 12 months
  • be able to read a car number plate from 20 metres
  • not be banned from driving
{{ govukInsetText({ text: "It costs £34 to apply online. You'll need a debit or credit card." }) }}

You'll need to sign in to your GOV.UK account to use this service. If you do not have an account, you can create one when you sign in.

You'll be asked to verify your identity using a UK passport if you have one. If you do not have a passport, you can still apply online but you may need to post original identity documents separately.

Other ways to apply

You can also apply by post using a D1 form. Collect one from a Post Office. Postal applications cost £43 and take around 3 weeks to process.

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
